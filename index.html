Discover More Live Music

See More Shows

With increased awareness of the variety of shows happening on any given night, you can go checkout new artists.

Fill Your Calendar

Save the shows you want to see to your calendar and you’ll be amazed by how quickly it fills up.

Dance with Friends

Invite friends to a particular show so you can enjoy it together. Share your entire show calendar with them so they won’t miss a beat!

Listen First

Interested in a show but never heard of who’s playing? Listen to some samples from the artist and checkout their bio and other information right in the app.

Follow Your Favorites

If you like what you hear, follow the artist so that you can keep track of when and where they’ll be playing next time.

Buy Tickets

Secure your tickets right in the app before the show sells out. Show your digital ticket at the door without any printing or hassle.
